about

This site acts as a journal for ideas and projects relating to my work and other interests.

I am a data science lead with experience across industries and problem spaces. Many projects involve working closely with product and company leadership at a strategic level to realise the potential of data & AI. This can range from facilitating data-informed decision making to driving the creation of innovative applications. You can find out more on my LinkedIn profile.

I’m particularly interested in how human-centric design and data science can be be brought together to produce experiences at their intersection.

In addition to that, I’m also interested in how processes and systems of work can actively foster creativity, particularly within interdisciplinary environments.

While not working, I’m involved in these projects:

  • A GIVEN B, a collaborative initiative aiming to bridge the gap between data science and design folks to improve how we work together.
  • Hot Concept Music, a new digital-only electronic and alternative music label, which I help in the running of.
  • NoNRG, an ambient music mix series with sporadic activity.

You can also find me on Twitter, where I lurk and tweet occasionally.

privacy policy

I value your privacy. While I use Google Analytics (GA) to understand traffic behaviour to this site, I ensure your IP is anonymised within GA, that “Do Not Track” settings are respected, and disable the use of cookies and only use session storage, which isn’t persistent, to store the GA Client ID.

The site is hosted on Github Pages and they will log information about you - you should consult their privacy policy in turn to learn more.
