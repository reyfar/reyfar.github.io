About

I’m a Data Science lead, currently based in Berlin. Projects I’ve been involved in cover the full range of data science areas, including machine learning (or “AI” as it is fashionably known), product analytics, business intelligence, data engineering and so on. I’ve worked across numerous sectors and for companies ranging from high-growth startups to global corporates. You can find further details on LinkedIn.

I’m particularly interested in product optimisation strategies and decision-making using data, as well as novel applications of machine learning.

Other interests include sound and music, and tinkering with software that generates them.

I’m also involved in these projects:

  • A given B, aka A|B, which is a collaborative effort I’m kicking off to encourage closer links between data science and designers to improve how we work together.
  • NoNRG, which is an ambient mix series hosted on SoundCloud with sporadic releases.

Thanks for reading!
