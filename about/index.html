About Me

This blog covers personal projects, work-related learnings and other random interests I’ve felt compelled to write about.

I live in Berlin and work as a Data Science consultant, with experience ranging across industries and problem spaces. You can find further details on my LinkedIn profile.

I’m also involved in these projects:

  • A GIVEN B which is a collaborative initiative to bridge the gap between data science and designers to improve how we work together.
  • NoNRG, which is an ambient mix series hosted on SoundCloud with sporadic releases.
