about

This site is intended to cover a range projects and ideas relating to my work and personal interests.

Professionally, I’m a principal data scientist with experience leading projects across industries and problem spaces, solving problems from implementing data-informed decision making to innovating with AI. You can find out more at LinkedIn.

I’m also involved in these projects:

  • AMP Unit, a collaboration between both machine learning experts and media theorists and practitioners, involving researchers from both Manchester Metropolitan University and University of Manchester.
  • A GIVEN B, an online initiative aiming to bridge the gap between data science and design folks to improve how we work together.
  • Hot Concept Music, a new digital-only electronic and alternative music label.

You can also find me on Twitter, where I lurk and very occasionally tweet.

Privacy policy

While I use Google Analytics (GA) to understand traffic behaviour to this site, I ensure your IP is anonymised within GA. I also set “Do Not Track” settings to be respected, disable the use of cookies and only use session storage, which isn’t persistent, to store the GA Client ID. Using this data I simply strive to get a rough idea of the scale of traffic and relative level of interest in content.

The site is hosted on Github Pages and they will mostly likely log information about you, which I cannot control. You should consult their privacy policy to learn more.
